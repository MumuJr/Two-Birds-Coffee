Our Story

It was three generations ago that our great-grandmother, the wife of a humble foreman, decided to start a small tailoring business in Guatemala. With her earnings and a sharp mind, she quickly recognized the that there was a market for buying and selling coffee between villages. After seeing some success, she saved up enough money to buy some land, and on that land, she started to plant coffee. That farm continued to grow through our grandfather and when he passed the land was split between his four children.
